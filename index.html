Nimets ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. 1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you create an account or use certain features, we may collect:

  • Account Information: Email address, username, password, and profile picture
  • User-Generated Content: Recipe comments, ratings, favorite recipes, and shopping lists
  • Communication Data: When you contact us via email, we collect your email address and message content

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, we automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features accessed, recipes viewed, search queries, time spent in the app
  • Analytics Data: App performance, crash reports, and diagnostic information
  • Location Data: General location based on IP address (not precise location)

1.3 Information from Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database storage, analytics, and cloud messaging
  • Google Sign-In: Name, email address, and profile picture (if you choose to sign in with Google)
  • Apple Sign In: Name and email address (if you choose to sign in with Apple)
  • RevenueCat: Subscription status and purchase history
  • Google AdMob: Advertising identifiers and ad interaction data

2.5 Advertising

  • Displaying relevant advertisements (with your consent for personalized ads)
  • Measuring ad performance and effectiveness
  • Complying with advertising regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)

3. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

3.1 Tracking Permission

On iOS 14.5 and later, we request your permission before tracking you across apps and websites owned by other companies. This permission is requested through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework.

3.2 What "Tracking" Means

Tracking refers to linking data collected in our App with data collected from other companies' apps or websites for targeted advertising or advertising measurement purposes.

3.3 Your Choices

  • Allow Tracking: You will receive personalized advertisements based on your interests
  • Ask App Not to Track: You will still see advertisements, but they will not be personalized based on your activity across other apps and websites

You can change your tracking preference anytime in: iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking

4. Personalized Advertising

4.1 Google User Messaging Platform (UMP)

Before showing ads, we request your consent through Google's User Messaging Platform to comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations.

4.2 Ad Personalization Choices

  • Consent Given: Ads will be personalized based on your interests and activity
  • Consent Denied: Ads will be non-personalized (contextual only)

4.3 Managing Ad Preferences

You can manage your advertising preferences:

  • In the App: Settings > Privacy Settings
  • iOS Settings: Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising
  • Google Ad Settings: https://adssettings.google.com

5. Auto-Renewable Subscriptions

5.1 Subscription Details

Monthly Subscription:

  • Price: €2.99 per month (prices may vary by region)
  • Billing Cycle: Monthly, renews automatically every month
  • Free Trial: Not currently offered

Yearly Subscription:

  • Price: €29.99 per year (prices may vary by region)
  • Billing Cycle: Yearly, renews automatically every year
  • Free Trial: 7-day free trial (may vary by promotion)

5.2 Auto-Renewal

Subscriptions automatically renew unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Your account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period.

5.3 Managing Subscriptions

You can manage and cancel your subscriptions at any time:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone or iPad
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Select Nimets
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription

Alternatively:

  • Open the App Store app
  • Tap your profile icon in the top right
  • Tap Subscriptions
  • Select Nimets and manage your subscription

5.4 Cancellation Policy

When you cancel:

  • You will continue to have access to premium features until the end of your current billing period
  • No refund will be provided for the remaining time in your current billing period
  • You will not be charged for subsequent billing periods

5.5 Refund Policy

Refunds are handled by Apple according to their policies. To request a refund:

  1. Visit https://reportaproblem.apple.com
  2. Sign in with your Apple ID
  3. Find the Nimets subscription purchase
  4. Follow the prompts to request a refund

We do not have direct access to process refunds for App Store purchases.

7. Data Security

7.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: Data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted using TLS/SSL
  • Firebase Security Rules: Restrict database access to authorized users only
  • Authentication: Secure authentication via Firebase Auth, Google Sign-In, and Apple Sign In
  • Access Controls: Limited employee access to user data on a need-to-know basis

7.2 Data Storage

Your data is stored on:

  • Firebase Cloud Firestore: Recipe data, user profiles, comments, and ratings
  • Firebase Storage: Profile pictures and recipe images
  • Your Device: Cached recipes and images for offline access

7.3 Important Notice

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Data Retention

8.1 How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • User-Generated Content: Comments and ratings retained indefinitely unless you delete them
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated analytics data retained for up to 26 months
  • Crash Reports: Retained for up to 90 days
  • Advertising Data: Subject to Google AdMob's retention policies (typically 60 days for identifiable data)

8.2 After Account Deletion

When you delete your account:

  • Personal information (email, username, profile picture) is deleted within 30 days
  • Comments are anonymized (username removed, content may remain)
  • Favorites and shopping lists are immediately deleted
  • Some data may be retained in backups for up to 90 days for disaster recovery
